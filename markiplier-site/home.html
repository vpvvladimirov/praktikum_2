About Markiplier

Mark Edward Fischbach, known online as Markiplier, is an American YouTuber, gamer, and philanthropist. He is best known for his playthroughs of horror games like Five Nights at Freddy’s and his engaging, comedic personality.

Born on June 28, 1989, in Honolulu, Hawaii, Markiplier started his YouTube career in 2012. Since then, he has amassed millions of subscribers and has contributed to numerous charity streams, raising millions for good causes.

What is he popular for?

Markiplier is widely recognized for his let's play videos, especially horror games. His Five Nights at Freddy’s playthroughs gained him massive popularity. He has also created original content such as "A Heist with Markiplier" and "In Space with Markiplier," interactive YouTube projects.

Beyond gaming, he co-founded the clothing brand Cloak alongside Jacksepticeye and has worked on various creative and charity projects.

Newest Project: Iron Lung

Markiplier is currently working on his latest movie, Iron Lung, an adaptation of the indie horror game of the same name. The movie follows the terrifying journey of a convict exploring an ocean of blood in a claustrophobic submarine. With Markiplier directing and starring, the film is set to bring his signature horror storytelling to the big screen.
